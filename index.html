HAPPINESS, GDP & Life Expectancy

This visualisation explores which Countries in 2020 have the most life satisfaction and how does this correlate to the nation's GDP per Capita and the life expectancy of the people of the nation

Happiness of Countries

The Choropleth Map on the left shows the Life Satisfaction of the Countries in the world in year 2020.


It shows that Finland is the happiest country in the world, the 2nd happiest is followed by Denmark .


Whereas, Afghanistan is the most unhappy country in the world, The 2nd most unhappy country is Lebanon.

Happiness vs GDP of countries

The Bubble Plot on the right shows the relationship between the Life Satisfaction and the GDP per capita of the Countries in the world.


Countries which plot in red is located in North America, plot in purple is located in South America, plot in orange is located in Europe, plot in light-blue is located in Africa, plot in dark blue is located in Asia, whereas plot in brown is located in Oceania.
